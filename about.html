About LIMB

The objective of the project is to design and develop a microprocessor-controlled (MCU) prosthetic arm with a remote monitoring web app for below the elbow limb loss. The website application allows users to access their past EMG data and see live EMG data as well. We also added 2 temperature sensors to allow users to know the internal temperature of their arm to ensure it is functioning properly and an external temperature sensor to mimic human touch capabilities. Below we have linked our GitHub and final report that provides greater insight in our how we completed this project.

We are grateful to have been given the opportunity to apply what we have learned in our biomedical engineering program to this final project before graduating in April 2021.
